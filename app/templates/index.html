Advanced Brain Tumor Detection Using AI

Our cutting-edge deep learning models revolutionize the way brain tumors are detected, segmented, and analyzed using MRI scans. Powered by UNet, ResNeXt50, and Feature Pyramid Networks, our solution achieves high accuracy while providing AI-assisted treatment recommendations with our integrated LLM system.

Advancing Healthcare Through AI

Our Advanced Models

We utilize three powerful deep learning architectures: Vanilla UNet, UNet with ResNeXt50 backbone, and Feature Pyramid Network for precise tumor segmentation with over 95% accuracy.

Research & Development

Our team continuously improves our models through rigorous research, experimentation, and validation using diverse datasets to ensure robustness and generalizability.

Global Impact

Brain tumors affect millions globally, with over 300,000 new cases diagnosed annually. Early detection is critical for improving treatment outcomes and patient survival rates.

MRI Analysis & Segmentation

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed images of the brain's soft tissues, making it the gold standard for brain tumor detection. Our AI system works by:

  • Processing raw MRI scans from standard DICOM formats
  • Pre-processing images to enhance feature visibility
  • applying our ensemble of deep learning models for tumor segmentation
  • Generating precise tumor masks that highlight location, size, and boundaries
  • Leveraging our LLM to provide interpretable insights and treatment suggestions

The result is a comprehensive analysis that helps physicians make better-informed decisions in seconds rather than hours.
