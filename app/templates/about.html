About BrainScan AI

We are a team of passionate researchers, developers, and healthcare professionals dedicated to revolutionizing brain tumor detection through artificial intelligence. Our mission is to make accurate diagnosis accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Story

BrainScan AI was founded in 2024 by a group of neuroscience researchers and AI specialists who recognized the need for more accurate and efficient brain tumor detection methods. What began as a research project quickly evolved into a mission to transform healthcare diagnostics globally.

Today, we've grown to a team of 30+ professionals spanning multiple disciplines, united by the goal of saving lives through technology. Our solutions are currently used in over 50 hospitals worldwide, helping thousands of patients receive faster and more accurate diagnoses.

Our Mission & Technology

At BrainScan AI, we're driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to make early and accurate brain tumor detection accessible to everyone, regardless of geographic or economic constraints. Brain tumors affect millions worldwide, with over 300,000 new cases diagnosed annually. Early detection is critical, often meaning the difference between life and death.

Our technology leverages three powerful deep learning architectures:

1. Vanilla UNet: Our baseline model provides exceptional segmentation capabilities through its contracting and expansive paths, maintaining high-resolution features through skip connections.

2. UNet with ResNeXt50 Backbone: By incorporating the ResNeXt architecture as our encoder, we achieve improved feature extraction with better handling of complex patterns in MRI images.

3. Feature Pyramid Network (FPN): This architecture helps us detect tumors at different scales, ensuring that both small and large abnormalities are accurately identified.

What sets us apart is our integrated LLM (Large Language Model) system that translates complex model outputs into clear, actionable insights for healthcare providers. This combination of advanced segmentation and interpretability makes our solution not just a detection tool, but a comprehensive diagnostic assistant.

Our research is ongoing, with continuous improvements in model accuracy, reduction in false positives, and expansion to different types of brain abnormalities. We collaborate with leading research institutions and hospitals to ensure our technology remains at the cutting edge while addressing real clinical needs.

By combining AI excellence with medical expertise, we aim to transform the standard of care for brain tumor patients worldwide.
